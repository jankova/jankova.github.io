Research interests

My research interests are primarily in the area of high-dimensional data analysis and machine learning. I work on developing methodology for statistical inference and estimation in sparse regression, graphical modeling, eigenstructure estimation or classification.

Bio

I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at Statistical Laboratory advised by Richard Samworth. Prior to joining the University of Cambridge, I was a PhD student at the Seminar for Statistics, ETH Zurich where I was fortunate to work with Sara van de Geer on statistical inference for high-dimensional data. I obtained my BSc and MSc Degrees from the Free University in Amsterdam and Comenius University in Bratislava.
